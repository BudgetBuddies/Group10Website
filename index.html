Introduction to Streaming

What is Streaming?

What is streaming?

The continuous transfer of audio and video files from a server to a client is referred to as "streaming." In video streams, information is transmitted over the internet in a compressed form and is instantly viewed by the viewer. A constant stream of data is used to send the material, which is played as it comes in. In simple words, Streaming is a form of media that can be accessed without a download. It can also refer to a form of media sharing done live or recorded, usually done by cameras broadcasting live to the internet.

What are the pros and cons of Live Streaming?

One of the biggest advantages of live-streaming is that you’re able to interact with the person you’re watching. Likewise, the streamer can also interact with their viewers' comments live. This helps build the relationship between the streamer and their community of viewers. On the other hand, some of the disadvantages of live streaming are that you constantly need stable wifi to view the stream or watch it in a decent and enjoyable quality. Accessibility also proves to be an obstacle as streaming can only be viewed online and with a device. Other people may not have access to devices to view this content.

What makes a good stream?

Many categories of content can be streamed. Some of these include music, documentaries, movies, educational content, gaming media, tutorials, and so much more which can all be uploaded to platforms such as YouTube and Facebook. Research and observations indicate that being a good streamer does not solely rely on the content you produce. It also heavily relies on how you interact with your audience. Many streamers have discovered that interacting with your community outside of the stream chat and live streams are ideal. A good stream is a stream that focuses on quality, interactivity, and relevance of content.

Frequently Asked Questions

The following are the questions often come into mind of our viewers.

Why financial literacy?

We chose this topic as it is a unique and broad topic that many of our interests (Business, Nursing, Psychology, and history) can be integrated into. We also believe that many APEC students would be interested in learning financial literacy and its history. These students might want to start businesses, find a job on the side or simply want to know how to save their money effectively. Our collaborative research could be their first stepping stone to financial independence. This topic can also give a head start for students who are going to learn ABM in senior high as they learn numerous financial and business terms.

Why will you be streaming?

We will be streaming with the primary purpose of increasing the Financial Literacy of our audience and introducing them to financial world. We will also be streaming with the secondary purpose of studying the effect of newly acquired monetary knowledge among various aspects of our viewers lives. This will help add to the research we are conducting.

Why did you document it through a website?

Documenting our journey on a website is much easier and more efficient compared to documenting on papers. It also allows us to better visualize and present our journey. Furthermore, a website increases interactivity, security, and accessibility for users

What can us viewers expect in your stream?

We can confidently say that in our future stream, you can expect to learn about the importance of financial literacy, budgeting and budgeting methods, investing and types of investments, and many other aspects of the financial world.



About Us

Good day! We are Budget Buddies, Group 10 of B09-10-S2-1, from APEC Ortigas Extension.

Budget Buddies is a group of researchers who aim to study the effect of Financial Literacy among high-school students to add to our case study thesis; "The Numerous Effects of Learning Financial Literacy Among the High School Students of APEC Schools Ortigas Extension Conducted in the Year 2022-2023". We will soon be conducting a webinar-like stream, educating our viewers on basic monetary concepts under financial literacy which will allow us to measure the effect that being more financially literate has taken on our viewers.

This website serves as a platform for us to document our journey in both our path to streaming and continuing to write our thesis. Our future stream will also be fully documented on this website. To learn more about our team and our work, scroll down, and join us on our journey.
